About Me

I'm an engineer, currently working at a company focused on industrial machinery. My work touches various segments of daily life, including medical applications as well as food packaging. While I originally studied Mechanical Engineering and have experience designing many different types of machines, I’ve found that my true passion lies in programming and working with data.


In my current role I automate and optimize processes every day. Using Python, SQL, VBA, and more, I create tools that make my team more effective. I’ve written programs to transform and clean data so that it’s ready for analysis, and I've written programs to generate regular insight reports. I also routinely create dashboards for displaying information and organizing findings.


I live in St. Petersburg, Florida with my beautiful wife and two dogs. I'm always learning something new, and I'm excited to see where the world of engineering and innovation will take me.
